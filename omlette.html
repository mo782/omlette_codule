simple omlette recipe

An easy and quick dish,perfect for any meal.This classic omlette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection,optionally filled with your choice of cheese,vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total:Approximatly 10 min.

  • Preparation:5 min.

  • Cooking:5 min.

ingredients

  • 2-3 large eegs.

  • Salt to taste.

  • Pepper to taste.

  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil.

  • Optional fillings:cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs.

instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: in a bowl, beat the eggs, with a pinch os salt and pepper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for fluffer texture.

  2. Heat the ban: A nun-stick frying pan over medium heat with butter or oil.

  3. Cook the omlette: pour on the eggs on the melted butter, and make sure you tilt the pan well so egg is coating all surface.

  4. Add fillings: springle your chosen fillings over one half of the omlette.

  5. Fold and serve: as the omlette continues to cook, lift one edge and fold it carefully over the filling, let it cook for one minute then slide it onto a plate.

  6. Enjoy: serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
